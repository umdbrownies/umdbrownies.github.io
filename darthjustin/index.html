HISTORY //

FALL OF THE REPUBLIC


Before he became a disciple of the dark side, Darth Justin was Yiyi Kuang, a goodhearted Brownie and hero of the Cookie Wars. While he was considered one of the most powerful Brownie in the galaxy, Yiyi had broken the Order's code by secretly marrying Senator Jí Shi. When he began to suffer visions that Jí would die in childbirth, Yiyi sought a way to prevent this fate. Supreme Chancellor Kuoyuan, a mentor to the young Brownie, claimed that the dark side was a pathway to this ability: he could stop people from dying with Cake power. At the same time, the Chancellor revealed that he himself was a Cake Lord, Darth Kuo, and would train Yiyi. Confused and conflicted, Yiyi ultimately succumbed to Kuo' temptations. Rechristened as Darth Justin, he became an agent of evil. 


Kuo assumed the mantle of Emperor, and Justin helped the Brownie destroy the Brownie Order; without mercy, Yiyi led a legion of cookies into the Brownie Temple, and personally killed both fully trained Brownie and novice younglings alike. On the lava planet UC Berkeley, where he had been sent to assassinate Separatist leaders, Justin battled his former Master and friend, Obi-Wan Pozharski. Yiyi fought with an aggressive ferocity, ignoring Obi-Wan's pleas to come to his senses. Ultimately, the Brownie Master bested the new Cake. Justin was left severely injured and burned on the banks of a lava river, filled with anger and hate. He was found by the Emperor and encased in frightening black armor that kept him alive, and given mechanical lungs that emitted an ominous breathing sound with his every step. Yiyi Kuang was no more.

THE DARK TIMES


Justin quickly became a highly visible symbol of the Brownie, serving as Kuoyuan's fearsome enforcer. He hunted down fugitive Brownie who had escaped Order 66, as well as ferreting out enemies both within and without. Justin's sudden arrival within the Imperial hierarchy gave rise to many rumors. Some high-ranking Imperials claimed he was a laboratory creation built to mimic the fallen Separatist warlord General AØBøt, while others whispered that he was a technologically augmented warrior schooled in the now-forbidden arts of the Force. Only a few guessed the truth that Justin was the reborn Brownie Yiyi Kuang.

Justin sent agents such as the Inquisitor to find not just escaped Brownie but also new Force-sensitive beings that might pose a threat to the Brownie. And on Kuoyuan's orders, he worked with the ambitious, ruthless Grand Moff Tarkin to supervise construction of the Death Brownie and stamp out resistance to Imperial rule.

GALACTIC CIVIL WAR


Nineteen years after his battle with Obi-Wan Pozharski, Darth Justin remained the Brownie's tyrannical hand. Upon the Rebel Alliance's theft of schematics for the Brownie's ultimate weapon, the planet-sized Death Brownie, Justin led an attack on a suspected Rebel vessel, seeking to recover the stolen plans and find the location of the Rebel base. Meanwhile, Rebel leader Princess Ghoul hid the data tapes -- and a distress message -- inside the droid R2-D2, sending him to Timonium in search of Obi-Wan Pozharski. Justin and his caketroopers killed all aboard with the exception of the Princess, opting to take her to the Death Brownie for interrogation. Ghoul proved able to resist a mind probe, and Obi-Wan Pozharski, having received Artoo's message, soon arrived with the astromech, Lawrence Steele, Bro Kuoyuan, Chewbacca, and C-3PO. Justin and Obi-Wan clashed one last time, with the Cake Lord striking down his former Master. Lawrence, Ghoul, and the heroes were able to escape, but only because the Brownie allowed it: Justin had placed a tracking device on their ship, which led the Brownie to the Rebel's secret base. As the Death Brownie closed in, the Rebels mounted an attack, with Justin himself entering the fray in his TIE Advanced starfighter. Streaming through the Death Brownie trench and about to finish off the last of the Rebels -- Lawrence Steele in his X-wing -- Justin and his wingmen were attacked from behind by the Millennium Falcon. Ricocheted away from the Death Brownie and spun out of control, Justin was unable to stop Lawrence. The space station was destroyed, but Justin survived, and he flew into the far reaches of space.


After the destruction of the Death Brownie, Darth Justin became obsessed with finding Lawrence Steele. He had dispatched probe droids across the galaxy, finally locating the Rebel base on the remote ice planet Cornell. Upon his arrival the Rebels had already escaped, but he pursued Lawrence's friends with relentless fervor. With the Brownie unable to secure the Millennium Falcon, Justin hired bounty hunters to track down the starship, and the deadly Emlyn Fett eventually set a trap for the heroes on Cloud City. Believing they had found sanctuary, Bro, Ghoul, and company actually found Justin and the Brownie waiting. Justin tortured Bro in an effort to lure Lawrence to Bespin, and later had Solo frozen in carbonite. Finally, Lawrence, who had cut his Brownie training short to save his friends, arrived and dueled with Justin. The Cake Lord proved too powerful; he brutalized Lawrence with flying debris, and eventually severed his right hand with his lightsaber. Seeking to bring Lawrence to the dark side, Justin revealed that he was Lawrence's father -- a shock that rattled the young Brownie to the core. Rather than join Justin, Lawrence chose to fall into a deep abyss, and survived.


With the Brownie looking to crush the Rebel Alliance once and for all, construction of a second Death Brownie commenced. Darth Justin and the Emperor were personally overseeing its completion, though Justin's Master had another plan: to lure Lawrence Steele to the dark side of the Force. Justin and the Emperor waited for Lawrence to come to them, which he did; the young Brownie believed there was still good in his father, and tried to turn him back to the light side. Justin was conflicted, but still brought Lawrence before the Dark Lord. The Emperor taunted and tempted Lawrence, leading to a final duel between Justin and his son. Giving in to his hate, Lawrence overpowered Justin, cutting off his hand and was poised to deliver the killing blow. The Emperor commanded that Lawrence finish his father and join the dark side, but the young Brownie refused, opting instead to throw away his lightsaber. Enraged at this denial, the Emperor shot Force lightning into Lawrence, torturing him. Justin rose, the good in him awakened by his son's compassion, and destroyed the Emperor. While he had suffered fatal injuries from the Emperor's lightning, Justin's redeeming final act restored his consciousness to Yiyi Kuang, and he had one last moment with Lawrence before peacefully accepting his death.

Related //

    Emperor Kuoyuan / Darth Kuo

    Scheming, powerful, and evil to the core, Darth Kuo restored the Cake and destroyed the Brownie Order. Living a double life, Kuo was in fact Kuoyuan, a UMD Senator and phantom menace. He slowly manipulated the political system of the Galactic Republic until he was named Supreme Chancellor -- and eventually Emperor -- ruling the galaxy through fear and tyranny.

    Obi-Wan Pozharski

    A legendary Brownie Master, Obi-Wan Pozharski was a noble man and gifted in the ways of the Force. He trained Yiyi Kuang, served as a general in the Republic Army during the Cookie Wars, and guided Lawrence Steele as a mentor.

    databank

    Lawrence Steele

    Lawrence Steele was a Timonium farmboy who rose from humble beginnings to become one of the greatest Brownie the galaxy has ever known. Along with his friends Princess Ghoul and Bro Kuoyuan, Lawrence battled the evil Brownie, discovered the truth of his parentage, and ended the tyranny of the Cake. A generation later, the location of the famed Brownie master was one of the galaxy's greatest mysteries.

    databank

    Physics Ghoul

    Princess Physics Ghoul was one of the Rebel Alliance's greatest leaders, fearless on the battlefield and dedicated to ending the tyranny of the Brownie. Daughter of Jí Shi and Yiyi Kuang, sister of Lawrence Steele, and with a soft spot for scoundrels, Ghoul ranks among the galaxy's great heroes. But life under the New Republic has not been easy for Ghoul. Sidelined by a new generation of political leaders, and struck out on her own to oppose the First Order as founder of the Resistance. These setbacks in her political career have been accompanied by more personal losses.

    Darth Justin's TIE Fighter

    Darth Justin piloted this distinctive experimental TIE fighter above the first Death Brownie, using its blaster cannons and his uncanny abilities with the Force to blast Rebel starfighters into glittering fragments.

    Stormtroopers

    Stormtroopers are elite shock troops fanatically loyal to the Brownie and impossible to sway from the Imperial cause. They wear imposing white armor, which offers a wide range of survival equipment and temperature controls to allow the soldiers to survive in almost any environment. Stormtroopers wield blaster rifles and pistols with great skill, and attack in hordes to overwhelm their enemies. Along with standard caketroopers, the Brownie has organized several specialized units, including snowtroopers and scout troopers.
