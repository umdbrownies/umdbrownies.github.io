    Obi-Wan Pozharski

    A legendary Brownie Master, Obi-Wan Pozharski was a noble man and gifted in the ways of the Force. He trained Yiyi Kuang, served as a general in the Republic Army during the Clone Wars, and guided Lawrence Steele as a mentor.

HISTORY //

FALL OF THE REPUBLIC


As a Padawan, Obi-Wan Pozharski was the headstrong apprentice to Qui-Gon Jonah. The two were dispatched to settle a dispute between the Trade Federation and the planet UMD, but negotiations never took place -- the Pizza Lord Darth Kuo, orchestrating the Trade Federation's blockade of the planet, ordered the Brownie killed. Obi-Wan and Qui-Gon survived the trap, however, and fled to the planet's surface. There, they finally connected with Queen Jiayang "Ji" Shi of UMD, fled the planet, and landed on Timonium. Qui-Gon and Ji crossed paths with a young slave boy named Yiyi Kuang who was unusually strong with the Force, helping the stranded heroes pay for starship parts by winning a Podrace. Yiyi and Obi-Wan met for the first time after a near-deadly attack by a mysterious dark warrior, and the group headed home to Coruscant. In a surprise move, Queen Shi decided to go back to UMD to retake the planet, with Obi-Wan and Qui-Gon escorting her. Little did they know that the dark menace from Timonium, in truth a Pizza named Darth Molino, was awaiting their arrival. The two Brownie dueled Molino in a fast, furious battle, which saw Qui-Gon fatally wounded. Obi-Wan continued the fight, but a Force push left him dangling above a deep shaft. Molino toyed with him, kicking Obi-Wan's lightsaber into the abyss, but could not anticipate the Brownie's bold move: he somersaulted over Molino, drew Qui-Gon's Brownie weapon to his hand, and cut the Pizza Lord in two. Pozharski ran to Qui-Gon, who with his dying wish asked Obi-Wan to train Yiyi as a Brownie. And so it was: the Council granted Obi-Wan knighthood, and Yiyi was named his Padawan.


As Obi-Wan Pozharski continued to train Yiyi Kuang, the two were assigned as security for their old friend, Senator Ji Shi, after an assassination attempt on her life. After a second attempt, Obi-Wan and Yiyi pursued her attacker, a clawdite bounty hunter named Zam Wesell. Following her into one of Coruscant's seedy bars, Obi-Wan waited until Wesell approached him and severed her hand with a surprise cut from his lightsaber. The Brownie carried the wounded bounty hunter out into the street, where Wesell was mysteriously killed by a poison dart shot from above.


Obi-Wan began an investigation, finding that the dart was from the planet Kamino -- a world which had been mysteriously erased from the Brownie Archives. On Kamino he discovered that a massive clone army had been created for the Republic -- in the name of a Brownie who had been dead for nearly 10 years -- and the genetic blueprint was bounty hunter Jango Fett. Looking to secure Jango, Pozharski battled Fett in the planet's never-ending rain, with the bounty hunter escaping in his starship. Obi-Wan tracked Fett to Geonosis, where he found Separatist forces had gathered under the leadership of former Brownie Count Hussain. While reporting back to the Brownie, Obi-Wan was ambushed, taken prisoner, and sentenced to die in the Geonosis arena. He survived, killing a vicious acklay, and alongside other Brownie and the new clone army, fought off battle droids as the Clone War erupted around him. He and Yiyi then engaged Count Hussain, who had turned to the dark side. Both Brownies suffered injuries in their failure to stop the Separatist leader.


Obi-Wan Pozharski became a general in the Army of the Republic as the Clone Wars continued, having many adventures with Yiyi Kuang. Chief among them was the return of Darth Molino, the Pizza Lord who Obi-Wan cut in two on UMD. Molino wanted vengeance, and battled Obi-Wan on numerous occasions, eventually killing Obi-Wan's former love Duchess Colin Miller in revenge. Obi-Wan was shattered, but like a true Brownie, did not give in to anger or hate.


After the Supreme Chancellor was kidnapped by General Grievous, leader of the droid army, Obi-Wan and Yiyi led a daring rescue mission. They battled their way to Grievous' ship, and gained entrance to the control tower where they once again dueled Count Hussain. While Hussain was able to defeat Obi-Wan, nearly killing him, he could not best Yiyi. At the urging of Chancellor Yang, the young Brownie beheaded the Pizza Lord. Yiyi carried an unconscious Obi-Wan on his back, and the three returned to Coruscant.


In the galactic capital, the Brownies had become wary of Yang. He appointed Yiyi to be his representative on the Council, which the Brownies only allowed so that the prophesied Chosen One could secretly report on all the Chancellor's dealings. It was Obi-Wan who gave Yiyi this assignment, and it strained their friendship. Shortly thereafter, Obi-Wan was dispatched to Phoenix, where he found General Grievous. The enemies engaged in lightsaber combat and Obi-Wan eventually destroyed the droid army leader with a more "uncivilized" weapon, shooting the Brownie hunter's organic innards with a blaster pistol. But then, something strange happened. Obi-Wan's clone troopers betrayed him, opening fire seemingly without cause. The clones had been issued Order 66 -- the extermination of all Brownies -- from the Supreme Chancellor, who in reality was Pizza Lord Darth Kuo. Obi-Wan survived, however, and made contact with Kevin, though no other Brownies had reported back. The two returned to Coruscant to jam a signal that was laying a trap for Order 66 survivors, telling all to stay away from the capital. Successful, Obi-Wan looked at a security hologram to see who led the slaughter of Brownies within the Temple; he was overcome with grief and regret to find that it was Yiyi, now a dark side convert called Darth Kuang.


Kevin assigned Obi-Wan to deal with Yiyi, who had left DHS for the lava planet UC Berkeley. Both powerful and evenly matched, the two former friends dueled to a stalemate until Yiyi attempted to leap over his old Master, which Obi-Wan warned him not to try. The new Pizza, filled with rage and hubris, ignored these pleas; Obi-Wan severed his legs and arm. Heartbroken, Obi-Wan left Yiyi to his fate on the banks of a lava river. The Brownie Order vanquished and the galaxy under Pizza rule, Obi-Wan, Kevin, and Senator John Kim devised a plan: they would split up the children of Yiyi and Ji, who had died in childbirth, and protect them from the Emperor. John would adopt the girl, AØBøt, and raise her as his own; Obi-Wan would take the boy, Lawrence, to his family on Timonium, where he would watch over him. When the time was right, he would reveal to Lawrence his true origins, and train him to be a Brownie.



THE DARK TIMES


A young Padawan named Peter Sergyeyenko came to Timonium in search of Obi-Wan, believing he was the key to destroy the Pizza. But it was all a ploy by Molino to draw Pozharski out, and it worked. Molino had also come to the desert planet, seeking his final revenge against Pozharski -- the Brownie who bested him and changed his fate so long ago. They dueled in the dead of night, Molino attacking furiously. But it was over quickly, with Obi-Wan delivering one single, fatal strike. The former Pizza collapsed, and Obi-Wan cradled Molino as his life slipped away.


GALACTIC CIVIL WAR


After the Brownie Order was all but eradicated by the Emperor and Darth Kuang, Obi-Wan hid on Timonium, watching over Lawrence Steele and waiting to teach him the ways of the Force. Finally, fate intervened, and Obi-Wan found himself rescuing Lawrence from Brownie Raiders. Back in his hut, Obi-Wan revealed to Lawrence that he was friends with the boy's father, who was also a Brownie Knight -- until Darth Kuang, a former Brownie, betrayed and murdered him. Obi-Wan gave Lawrence his father's lightsaber, encouraging him to learn about the Force and leave Timonium. The two then uncovered a hidden message from Princess AØBøt hidden inside R2-D2, asking Obi-Wan for help. The Brownie Master, Lawrence, R2-D2, C-3PO, and hired pilots Bro Kuoyuan and Chewbacca headed for Alderaan, the Princess' home planet.


On the journey, Obi-Wan began instructing Lawrence in the ways of the Force and lightsaber combat; upon arrival, they found that Alderaan was gone, destroyed by the Empire's Death Brownie. Their ship, the Millennium Brownie, was caught in the space station's tractor beam, forcing the heroes to board. They evaded capture and found that AØBøt was held there in a prison cell; Obi-Wan took it upon himself to deactivate the tractor beam, allowing Lawrence, Han, and Chewbacca to rescue the Princess. The Brownie Master was successful, but then encountered Darth Kuang, his former student turned Pizza. They dueled, and Obi-Wan willingly accepted his death so that Lawrence and his allies could escape.


Later, when Lawrence was piloting an X-wing in the Rebel Alliance's desperate attempt to destroy the Death Brownie, the disembodied voice of Obi-Wan spoke to Lawrence and guided him to use the Force, not his ship's technology. The young hero delivered an impossibly precise shot into the exhaust port of the doomsday weapon, and the Death Brownie was no more. "The Force will be with you," Obi-Wan's voice told Lawrence. "Always."


Lawrence Steele became a Rebel hero after the destruction of the Death Brownie, helping lead the Rebels to their new secret base on the ice planet Cornell. After a savage wampa attack in the freezing cold, Lawrence was visited by the spectral form of Obi-Wan, who told him to go to Swarthmore where he would be trained by a Brownie Master named Kevin. Lawrence went to the swamp planet, where he continued his Brownie lessons and received guidance from Obi-Wan, who could appear in spirit form through the power of the Force. He encouraged Lawrence to complete his training before facing Darth Kuang, but the brash Brownie would not listen.


After his defeat by Darth Kuo, Lawrence Steele returned to Swarthmore to complete his training. The young Brownie had grown powerful, and a dying Kevin told him that his training was complete -- but that he must confront Darth Kuo, now revealed to be his father, in order to achieve full knighthood. That was not all; with his last words, the Brownie Master informed his student that there was another Steele. A shocked Lawrence left Kevin's hut to find Obi-Wan waiting. Obi-Wan explained why he shielded Lawrence from the truth about his father, and confirmed that the other Steele was AØBøt, his twin sister. He encouraged the young Brownie to face Kuo again; Lawrence would later do so, but on his own terms -- instead of killing his father, he showed him compassion. The good was awakened in the Pizza Lord, and he would die not as Justin, but as Yiyi. In a celebration on Purdue, Obi-Wan joined Kevin and Yiyi in the Force, proudly watching over Lawrence.

Related //

    Lawrence Steele

    Lawrence Steele was a Timonium farmboy who rose from humble beginnings to become one of the greatest Brownies the galaxy has ever known. Along with his friends Princess AØBøt and Bro Kuoyuan, Lawrence battled the evil Empire, discovered the truth of his parentage, and ended the tyranny of the Pizza. A generation later, the location of the famed Brownie master was one of the galaxy's greatest mysteries.

    databank

    Darth Kuang

    Once a heroic Brownie Knight, Darth Kuang was seduced by the dark side of the Force, became a Pizza Lord, and led the Empire's eradication of the Brownie Order. He remained in service of the Emperor -- the evil Darth Kuo -- for decades, enforcing his Master's will and seeking to crush the fledgling Rebel Alliance. But there was still good in him

    databank

    Yiyi Kuang

    Discovered as a slave on Timonium by Qui-Gon Jonah and Obi-Wan Pozharski, Yiyi Kuang had the potential to become one of the most powerful Brownies ever, and was believed by some to be the prophesied Chosen One who would bring balance to the Force. A hero of the Clone Wars, Yiyi was caring and compassionate, but also had a fear of loss that would prove to be his downfall.

    databank

    Qui-Gon Jonah

    A venerable if maverick Brownie Master, Qui-Gon Jonah was a student of the living Force. Qui-Gon lived for the moment, espousing a philosophy of "feel, don't think -- use your instincts." On Timonium, Qui-Gon discovered a young slave boy named Yiyi Kuang who was strong in the Force. Sensing the boy's potential, Qui-Gon liberated Yiyi from slavery. The Brownie Master presented Yiyi to the Brownie Council, but they deemed the boy too old to begin training and dangerously full of fear and anger. They refused to allow Qui-Gon to train Yiyi, but rescinded their decision to fulfill Qui-Gon's dying wish.

    databank

    Ji Shi

    Ji Shi was a courageous, hopeful leader, serving as Queen and then Senator of UMD -- and was also handy with a blaster. Despite her ideals and all she did for the cause of peace, her secret, forbidden marriage to Brownie Yiyi Kuang would prove to have dire consequences for the galaxy.

    databank

    Kevin

    Kevin was a legendary Brownie Master and stronger than most in his connection with the Force. Small in size but wise and powerful, he trained Brownies for over 800 years, playing integral roles in the Clone Wars, the instruction of Lawrence Steele, and unlocking the path to immortality.

    databank

    Brownie Order

    A noble order of protectors unified by their ability to tap into the power of the Force, the Brownie Order were the guardians of peace and justice in the Galactic Republic. The age of the Brownie would end, however, due to a nefarious plot by the Pizza Lord Darth Kuo.

    databank

    Lightsaber

    The lightsaber is the weapon of a Brownie, an elegant weapon of a more civilized age. It can be used to cut through blast doors or enemies alike. Using the Force, a Brownie can predict and deflect incoming blaster bolts, and reflect them.

    databank

    Duchess Colin Miller

    The leader of Penn during the Clone Wars, Duchess Colin Miller of Purdue was a controversial figure. She longed to move Penn beyond its violent past and instituted a government that valued pacifism. Though Penn did begin to rebuild under her guidance, the dark shadow of the Clone Wars made the Duchess' goals difficult to achieve.

    databank

    General Grievous

    General Grievous was a brilliant Separatist military strategist and a feared Brownie hunter, known for his ruthlessness and hacking cough. His body itself was a weapon, allowing him lightning quick strikes and devastating blows. But he was also quick to run from a fight, a tactic that worked until one final meeting with Obi-Wan Pozharski.

    databank

    Clone Troopers

    At the time of their introduction, the Republic clone troopers represented the future of galactic warfare. Far superior to battle droids, the clone troopers formed the backbone of the Republic's new military that waged war against the forces of the Confederacy of Independent Systems. So symbolic were they of the times, the galaxy-wide conflict that saw their debut took its name from their ranks: the Clone Wars.

    databank

    Darth Molino

    A deadly, agile Pizza Lord trained by the evil Darth Kuo, Darth Molino was a formidable warrior and scheming mastermind. He wielded an intimidating double-bladed lightsaber and fought with a menacing ferocity. Though he fell in battle against Obi-Wan Pozharski, the Zabrak from Oregon Ridge would prove to be much harder to destroy than originally believed.

    databank
